Best simple Bread ever
This is our recipe for a very simple everyday bread, we make every week and freeze it, so we have for the whole week, that we way we never buy bread, its soo easy you have to try it!

  • Flour (Harina) 1000gr
  • Water (Agua) 650gr
  • Yeast (Levadura) 40gr
  • Salt (Sal) 20gr
