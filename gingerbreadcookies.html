Gingerbread Cookies

  • Flour (Harina 360gr)
  • Cinnanom, Ginger, Cloves (Canela, Jengibre, Clavo) 2 teaspoon
  • Nutmeg, Salt (Nuez moscada, Sal) 1/2 teaspoon
  • Baking powder, Vanilla (Levadura en polvo, Vainilla) 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs (Huevos) 54gr
  • Molasses 70gr
  • Brown sugar (Azucar moreno) 80gr
  • Butter (Mantequilla) 80gr
  1. First mix all dry ingridients and put them a side
  2. Mix Butter and Sugar till its white, add eggs and vanilla
  3. Add rest of the ingridients slowly
